HIV PREVENTION & MANAGEMENT

HIV Testing & Counselling • PrEP • PEP

Confidential HIV Testing and Prevention Services in Delmas At The Bears Healthcare Group, we provide confidential HIV prevention and management services in Delmas, focused on early detection, education, and ongoing support. Our clinic offers professional HIV testing, counselling, and preventative treatments in a safe and respectful medical environment where every patient is treated with dignity and privacy. Our HIV testing and counselling services are designed to give patients clear, accurate results together with compassionate guidance. Every test is accompanied by professional counselling to help individuals understand their results, learn about prevention methods, and receive support for making informed health decisions. For individuals at higher risk of exposure, we offer PrEP (Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis) — a highly effective medication that significantly reduces the risk of contracting HIV when taken consistently as prescribed. PrEP is an important prevention option for individuals who want to proactively protect their health. In situations where potential exposure may have already occurred, our clinic provides PEP (Post-Exposure Prophylaxis). This emergency prevention treatment must be started within 72 hours of exposure to help prevent the virus from establishing infection. At The Bears Healthcare Group, our goal is to empower individuals through education, prevention, and confidential medical care, ensuring that every patient has access to reliable information, supportive counselling, and professional healthcare in a stigma-free environment.
